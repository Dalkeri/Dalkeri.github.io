Multiplayer/Puzzle

Camera:

Top

Technology:

html5 with Airconsole

Estimated playtime:

10 mins

My Role:

On this project, I convinced my team to try to make a game with airconsole and I was in charge of the Game Design and the smartphone part, consisting in UI.

Battle Arena Revenge is a local multiplayer game, up to 4 players. You play with you phone, at the beginning, you connect your smartphone to Airconsole.com and type the code given by the game. This is a way to use your smartphone as a gamepad while you play on your computer or your TV. In this game, players select their moves on their phones then the game make the characters do the actions in the same order as specified by the players. When someone walk on a tile, it falls in the abyss. The last surviving player wins.
