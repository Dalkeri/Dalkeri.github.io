10 mins

My Role:

On this big project, I have started by helping the Game Designers at the beginning then I took a role of gameplay programmer. I coded some of the situations when Aarold challenges the player as well as few other things like the menus and integration of the scenario.

In Aarold and me!, you play with Aarold a former hero, he has done every challenges he could find, he is really powerful but for now, he just wants to rest in his village with his friends. One day, he receives a new mission and you, you will have to control him but this mission is too trivial. He will regularly challenge you to make his journey more interesting.
