My Role:

On this project, I was the only developer, I got the assets (except the sound) and had to conceive each part of the Game Design then code everything.

Tribal Attack is a shmup. You play a flying boat trying to explore the jungle but it is pretty hostile to strangers. You'll have to defeat waves of enemies until the final boss. I recommand watching how to play. There is one special feature I had to put in the game, I decided to do a power that allow you to put a holographic clone of the ship that will attract enemies' focus.
