Game Name:

Extrem Tube of Hell (EToH)

Genre:

Runner

Camera:

Third person

Platform:

Smartphone & watch (android only)

My Role:

For this game, we had to make a game in pairs for watches. I worked on the Game Design and the UI of this game and was hardly involved in the development part.

Extrem Tube of Hellis a runner, you have to go the most far possible in this twisted tube by dodging obstacles. This game was designed to be played easily on watches but this also works for any android phone.

Download

You can choose between 3 difficulties and you can see you best score for each difficulty. Swipe to the sides to change difficulty.
